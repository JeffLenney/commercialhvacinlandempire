Key Things to Know Before You Buy

  • Water-based kits contain 40–60% solids -- the rest evaporates and never becomes coating
  • Acid washing leaves inconsistent surface profiles that cause delamination
  • Hot tires can reach 150°F -- most DIY coatings have a heat deflection point far below that
  • UV from coastal sunlight yellows non-aliphatic top coats within 12–18 months
  • Moisture vapor in OC concrete slabs can blow off a coating if not tested first

What the ArmorClad System Solves
