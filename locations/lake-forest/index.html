Built for Lake Forest’s Range of Homes

From Baker Ranch’s New Slabs to El Toro’s Established Ones

Lake Forest spans a wide range of home ages. The newer Baker Ranch development (built 2013 and later) features clean, well-formed concrete slabs that are ready for a direct ArmorClad installation with proper diamond-grind preparation. Foothill Ranch and the older El Toro-era neighborhoods have slabs from the 1980s and ’90s that typically need crack assessment and remediation before coating.

We scale our approach to what the slab actually requires. That means Baker Ranch homeowners aren’t paying for remediation they don’t need, while older Foothill Ranch slabs get the thorough preparation that makes a coating last a decade rather than peel within two years.

Lake Forest’s inland position means summer garage temperatures climb well above what the coast experiences. Hot-tire resistance is a genuine concern - and a core feature of every ArmorClad installation we do in the area.

Diamond-Grind Prep for New & Old Slabs

New concrete has a surface laitance layer that must be removed before any coating adheres properly. Older slabs need mechanical profiling to open the concrete. Diamond grinding addresses both - it’s the correct preparation regardless of slab age. Learn more.

🏎️

Hot-Tire Resistance

Lake Forest sits inland where summer temperatures push garage floors well above what water-based coatings can handle. The ArmorClad 100% solids system is engineered to resist hot-tire pick-up - the most common failure point for cheaper coatings in South OC summers.

Our Process in Lake Forest

What to Expect

Every Lake Forest installation starts with an honest slab assessment. We tell you exactly what your concrete needs before we quote - no surprises, no hidden costs.

📋

1. Slab Assessment

We inspect for cracks, oil contamination, previous coatings, and moisture vapor emission. On Baker Ranch’s newer slabs this is straightforward; on older Foothill Ranch slabs we take extra care to identify any issues that need remediation.

💎

2. Diamond-Grind & Repair

Industrial diamond grinding to CSP 3 profile. Any cracks, spalls, or control joint damage are repaired with structural epoxy before the base coat goes down. No shortcuts on prep - it’s the foundation of everything.

✨

3. ArmorClad Installation

100% solids epoxy base, decorative chip or metallic broadcast in your chosen color, and UV-stable polyaspartic top coat. Most Lake Forest garages are completed in a single day.
