Built to Withstand Real-World Punishment

We designed the ArmorClad system to handle conditions that destroy conventional coatings. Here’s how it performs where it matters most.

🏎️

Hot-Tire Pick-Up Resistance

The ArmorClad 100% solids base coat is engineered to withstand thermal stress from hot tires on OC summer days. No lifting, no bubbling, no peeling - even with daily in-and-out parking of performance vehicles or trucks.

🛢️

Oil & Chemical Resistance

Motor oil, brake fluid, transmission fluid, power steering fluid, battery acid - the polyaspartic top coat resists all of them. Spills wipe clean rather than penetrating the surface and leaving permanent stains.

🧲

Impact & Abrasion Resistance

Dropped tools, bike kickstands, heavy shelving units - the ArmorClad system builds a coating that’s significantly harder and more abrasion-resistant than water-based alternatives.

🚿

Anti-Slip Safety Broadcast

A fine quartz aggregate broadcast into the base coat provides grip underfoot even when the surface is wet from car wash runoff, irrigation overspray, or rainy-day tracking.
