Built for the HB Lifestyle

The Surf City Garage Is a Working Space

Huntington Beach garages don’t just park cars. They store surfboards, hang wetsuits, rack bikes, and accumulate the sand, salt water, and gear of an active coastal lifestyle. That foot traffic - wet, sandy, and frequent - destroys standard floor coatings faster than almost anything else.

The ArmorClad system handles all of it. The 100% solids epoxy base bonds permanently to properly prepared concrete and resists moisture penetration from below and above. The UV-stable polyaspartic top coat handles the intense direct Pacific sun without yellowing or chalking. And the anti-slip quartz aggregate broadcast keeps the floor safe underfoot, even when dripping wetsuits and wet boards are involved.

From Huntington Harbour’s waterfront homes to Seacliff’s gated estates, Huntington Beach homeowners need a floor that can go from surfboard storage to showroom quality - and ArmorClad does exactly that.

The System Behind the Finish

Why the ArmorClad Industrial System
Is Built for Huntington Beach

Surf City’s combination of UV, salt air, moisture, and heavy use demands a coating system engineered for performance - not just appearance.

☀️

UV-Stable Polyaspartic Top Coat

Aliphatic polyaspartic chemistry is stable under direct UV by its molecular structure - not by additive. It won’t yellow, chalk, or lose gloss in HB’s intense coastal sun. The same chemistry is used in commercial coastal architecture.

🌊

Salt-Air & Moisture Resistance

The ArmorClad base coat creates a true vapor barrier once cured. Combined with moisture vapor emission testing before installation, we eliminate the delamination risk that plagues coastal epoxy installations done without proper assessment.

🦶

Anti-Slip Surface for Wet Conditions

Quartz aggregate broadcast into the base coat provides reliable grip underfoot - even when wet wetsuits, boards, and feet are crossing the floor. No more slip hazard from an overly polished surface.

🏎️

Hot-Tire Resistance

HB’s summer heat pushes coastal garage temperatures high. The ArmorClad system prevents hot-tire pick-up - the most common failure mode for cheaper coatings during Orange County summers. Learn more about hot-tire pickup.

Our Process in Huntington Beach

What to Expect

Every Huntington Beach installation follows the full ArmorClad 5-step protocol. Coastal conditions require every step - skipping surface prep or moisture testing in an HB garage is how failures happen.

💧

1. Moisture Vapor Testing

Coastal slabs often carry elevated moisture vapor emission rates. We test before we coat - every time - to ensure permanent adhesion in Huntington Beach’s humid environment.

💎

2. Diamond-Grind & Repair

Industrial diamond grinding to CSP 3 profile, followed by crack and spall remediation. The only preparation method that creates reliable adhesion in coastal concrete conditions.

✨

3. ArmorClad Coastal Installation

100% solids epoxy base with anti-slip quartz broadcast, plus UV-stable aliphatic polyaspartic top coat. Built to handle the specific demands of a Surf City garage.
