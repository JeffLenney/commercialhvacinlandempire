The Land of Gracious Living

Large Garages, Hot Summers, High Standards

Yorba Linda consistently ranks among Orange County’s most affluent cities, and it shows in the homes - large custom and semi-custom properties, expansive lots, and three- and four-car garages that often double as car storage for collectors, weekend vehicles, and enthusiast builds. These garages deserve a floor coating that matches the level of what’s being stored in them.

Yorba Linda also sits well inland, away from the coast’s moderating influence. Summer temperatures regularly push into the 95–105°F range, and garage floors absorb significant heat. This makes hot-tire pickup - the failure mode where a heated tire literally pulls up a cheap coating when the car moves - one of the most common problems in Yorba Linda garages. The ArmorClad system is engineered specifically to resist it.

For homes near the Yorba Linda Country Club, Black Gold Golf Club, or the hillside estates near the freeway, we offer premium metallic and solid-color broadcast options that create the kind of floor you see in high-end car dealerships and private collections.
