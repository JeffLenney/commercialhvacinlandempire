Why Coastal Environments Demand More

The UV and Salt-Air Challenge in Newport Beach

Newport Beach receives significantly more UV radiation than inland Orange County - and the salt air that rolls in off the Pacific accelerates degradation in coatings not specifically formulated for coastal conditions. Standard epoxies and water-based garage coatings yellow, chalk, and lose gloss within 12–24 months in this environment.

The ArmorClad system uses an aliphatic polyaspartic top coat - the same chemistry specified for commercial coastal architecture and marine environments. Aliphatic polyaspartics are UV-stable by chemistry, not by additive. They don’t yellow, don’t chalk, and retain their gloss and chemical resistance in direct sun exposure year after year.

For Newport Beach garages that house Porsches, Ferraris, Lamborghinis, and other appreciating assets, this isn’t a luxury - it’s a requirement for maintaining the value and appearance of your space.
