Ontario's Industrial HVAC Landscape

Ontario commands the most strategically positioned industrial real estate in the western United States. The convergence of Interstate 10 and Interstate 15 — known as the "IE Interchange" — anchors over 200 million square feet of industrial inventory within a 10-mile radius. Every major national logistics operator has a presence here.

Key Industrial Zones We Serve

  • Airport District (ONT): The area immediately surrounding Ontario International Airport is the densest concentration of logistics and air cargo facilities in the IE. We service multiple facilities within this corridor including major freight forwarding operations and express package hubs.
  • I-10/I-15 Interchange Cluster: The mile-radius around the I-10/I-15 interchange hosts dozens of Class A industrial buildings ranging from 500,000 to 2 million+ square feet. These high-cube warehouses require powerful, reliable RTU systems to maintain safe working temperatures.
  • Ontario Ranch / South Ontario: The newer industrial developments south of the 60 freeway and along Milliken Ave represent the next generation of Ontario logistics — modern buildings with sophisticated HVAC requirements and increasingly high ESG standards.
  • East Ontario / Archibald Corridor: A mix of mid-century manufacturing and newer logistics facilities along Archibald Ave and Grove Ave, including light manufacturing and assembly operations.

Major Employers & Facilities in Ontario

Ontario's industrial roster reads like a who's-who of American logistics. Our technicians work regularly in or near facilities operated by companies including Amazon (multiple fulfillment and sortation centers), Costco distribution, GEODIS logistics, Toyota Logistics Services, UPS, FedEx, DHL, and dozens of 3PL operators. We understand the operational demands — strict temperature windows, 24/7/365 operations, and zero tolerance for extended downtime.

Freeway Access & Response Coverage

Ontario's freeway network means our dispatch can reach virtually any facility in the city within 30 minutes during off-peak hours. We maintain service coverage for all areas accessible from I-10, I-15, SR-60, and SR-83, including the full Ontario industrial submarket from Milliken Ave to Euclid Ave, and from the 10 freeway north to Foothill Blvd.
