Riverside's Commercial HVAC Landscape

As Riverside County's seat and the home of UC Riverside, Riverside has long been the intellectual and administrative center of the county. In recent decades, it has also become an important logistics and industrial market — particularly along the SR-91 corridor and near the March Air Reserve Base / March Commerce Center area.

Key Zones We Serve

  • SR-91 Freeway Corridor: The State Route 91 freeway corridor through Riverside hosts major distribution and warehousing development, connecting to Orange County markets to the west and the broader IE to the east. Large-format industrial buildings along this corridor require high-capacity RTU systems to manage thermal loads in Riverside's hot-summer climate.
  • UC Riverside Campus Area: The University of California Riverside campus and surrounding research and technology park represent significant commercial HVAC demand. Research buildings, administrative facilities, and campus residential halls each have specific HVAC requirements. We service the broader UCR-area commercial buildings.
  • Riverside University Health System / Medical Campus: Riverside is home to significant medical infrastructure including the Riverside University Health System medical center and numerous specialty medical office buildings. Medical-grade HVAC requires strict humidity control, reliable redundancy, and prompt emergency response.
  • Downtown Riverside / Mission Inn District: Riverside's historic downtown includes the iconic Mission Inn, government buildings, retail, restaurants, and professional offices. Commercial HVAC in this older building stock often requires creative solutions for equipment access and building-specific integration.
  • March Commerce Center / East Industrial: The area near March Air Reserve Base hosts a growing industrial and logistics corridor, with warehouse development benefiting from proximity to the 215 freeway and March's industrial infrastructure.

Major Employers & Institutions

Riverside's top employers include Riverside University Health System, the University of California Riverside, Riverside Unified School District, the County of Riverside, major distribution and logistics operators along SR-91, and a growing tech and professional services sector near UCR. Our HVAC service programs are designed to support the operational continuity of each of these employer categories.
