Consistent Quality, Everywhere

The Same ArmorClad System, Every City

No matter which city you’re in, every Garage Pros OC installation uses the ArmorClad Industrial System - the same diamond-grind preparation, the same 100% solids epoxy base, and the same UV-stable polyaspartic top coat. What changes is the local knowledge we bring: HOA color requirements in Irvine, coastal chemistry in Newport Beach, aged-slab expertise in Fullerton.

Our network model means you get a local installer who works in your market every day, backed by a regional system with consistent standards, materials, and accountability.
