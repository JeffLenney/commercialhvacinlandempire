What Happens Next

The Assessment Process

  • 1

    Initial Contact

    We respond within one business day to confirm your details and schedule the site visit.

  • 2

    Site Engineering Assessment

    A specialist visits your property to measure sq footage, evaluate drainage slope, soil composition, sun exposure, and HOA restrictions.

  • 3

    System Specification

    We specify the exact turf product, infill type, sub-base depth, and drainage design for your application. No generic quotes.

  • 4

    Rebate Pre-Application

    If your city qualifies, we file your MWDOC pre-approval application before any work begins to lock in your rebate.

  • 5

    Transparent Quote

    You receive an itemized quote: materials, labor, sub-base, infill, and your projected rebate deducted — net cost clearly stated.
