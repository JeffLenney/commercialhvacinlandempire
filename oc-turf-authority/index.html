Face weight is the weight of the turf fibers per square yard, excluding the backing. Higher face weight (60–80+ oz) means more fiber density, better durability under foot traffic, and a more realistic appearance. We specify face weight to match the application — recreation and pet systems require higher face weights than purely decorative installs.
