ROI & Water Rebates

How to Get Your Turf for Free (or Close to It) in 2026

Orange County water districts are paying homeowners up to $4.00 per square foot to remove grass and install water-efficient alternatives. On a 2,000 sq ft backyard in Anaheim Hills, that’s $8,000 back before you factor in water savings.

Our MWDOC Application Process

The Municipal Water District of Orange County (MWDOC) administers turf conversion rebates for most OC cities. We handle the paperwork — you collect the check.

  • 1

    Site Assessment

    We measure your grass area and document irrigation system.

  • 2

    Pre-Application

    We submit MWDOC pre-approval before any work begins.

  • 3

    Product Compliance

    We spec from MWDOC’s approved product list to guarantee eligibility.

  • 4

    Installation

    Work begins only after written pre-approval is confirmed.

  • 5

    Post-Install Inspection

    We coordinate the final MWDOC inspection on your behalf.

  • 6

    Rebate Issued

    You receive your rebate check — typically 30–60 days post-inspection.
