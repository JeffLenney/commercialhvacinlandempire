Project Gallery

Three documented installations across Orange County, each showing the exact slab condition we found, how we solved it, and the finished result.

Irvine, CA

Epoxy Flooring in Irvine

Challenge

Years of vehicle storage had driven motor oil deep into the slab pores, creating a contamination layer that standard surface cleaners couldn’t break. Any coating applied over it would delaminate within months.

Solution

Multi-cycle industrial degreasing followed by diamond grinding to expose clean aggregate, then a penetrating epoxy primer engineered to displace residual hydrocarbons before the full ArmorClad base coat was applied.

Result

A permanently bonded, full-build ArmorClad surface in an HOA-approved charcoal flake blend, rated for daily vehicle traffic and over a decade of Southern California weather. See all Irvine epoxy flooring work.

Newport Beach, CA

Epoxy Flooring in Newport Beach

Challenge

The homeowner’s existing coating had turned amber within 18 months of installation, a sign of an aromatic epoxy topcoat that isn’t UV-stable. Salt air and direct coastal sun had accelerated the breakdown.

Solution

Complete removal of the failed coating, diamond grinding to expose fresh concrete, and a full ArmorClad rebuild using an aliphatic polyaspartic topcoat specifically rated for UV stability in Southern California coastal environments.

Result

A deep-gloss, UV-stable finish that won’t yellow, amber, or chalk, backed by ArmorPoxy’s long-term UV-stability rating and built to outlast the coastal conditions that failed the first coating. See all Newport Beach garage coatings.

Anaheim Hills, CA

Epoxy Flooring in Anaheim Hills

Challenge

Moisture vapor transmission (MVT) readings came in above 8 lbs/1,000 sq ft/24 hrs, more than double the 3 lb threshold at which standard epoxy systems begin to lose adhesion from hydrostatic pressure beneath the slab.

Solution

A two-part ArmorPoxy vapor barrier primer applied directly to the diamond-ground surface, sealing off the moisture drive before the standard ArmorClad full-build system was installed on top.

Result

A structurally sound coating with no bubbling, no delamination risk, and a finished surface built for the below-grade moisture conditions common in Anaheim Hills hillside homes.

Key Things to Know Before You Buy

  • Water-based kits contain 40–60% solids; the rest evaporates and never becomes coating
  • Acid washing leaves inconsistent surface profiles that cause delamination
  • Hot tires can reach 150°F, and most DIY coatings have a heat deflection point far below that
  • UV from coastal sunlight yellows non-aliphatic top coats within 12–18 months
  • Moisture vapor in OC concrete slabs can blow off a coating if not tested first

What the ArmorClad System Solves

  • 100% solids: every gram of wet coat becomes hard film
  • Diamond grinding to CSP 3: guaranteed mechanical adhesion
  • Engineered hot-tire resistance, tested to OC summer conditions
  • Aliphatic polyaspartic top coat: UV-stable for 15+ years
  • Pre-install moisture vapor testing: standard on every project
