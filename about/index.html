Our Core Material

The ArmorClad Industrial System

Every Garage Pros OC installation uses the ArmorClad system - a multi-coat floor platform engineered for environments that demand performance, not just appearance.

🔬

100% Solids Epoxy Base

The foundation of every ArmorClad installation. 100% solids means there are zero solvents or water in the formula - the entire wet coat becomes hard film. Maximum thickness, maximum bond strength, zero pinholes.

🛡️

Hot-Tire Pick-Up Resistance

The ArmorClad system is specifically engineered to handle the thermal stress of hot tires contacting the floor repeatedly. This is the number-one failure point for big-box epoxy kits - and a non-issue with ArmorClad.

☀️

UV-Stable Polyaspartic Top Coat

A pure polyaspartic aliphatic top coat seals every installation. It won’t amber or yellow in coastal sunlight, resists chemical spills, and creates the high-gloss or satin sheen that makes your floor look like a showroom.

Side-by-Side Comparison

Professional Installation vs. DIY Epoxy Kit

Big-box DIY kits can look similar in photos. The performance difference shows up in 12–18 months - or sooner on the first hot summer day.

Feature Garage Pros OC (ArmorClad) Big-Box DIY Epoxy Kit
Epoxy Chemistry 100% Solids - No Fillers Water-Based (40–60% solids)
Surface Preparation Diamond Grinding (CSP 3) Acid Wash (inconsistent adhesion)
Hot-Tire Resistance Engineered to resist peeling Will peel under repeated hot-tire contact
UV Stability UV-stable polyaspartic top coat Yellows and fades within 1–2 years
Crack & Spall Repair Included in every project Not included - voids visible through coating
Anti-Slip Broadcast Quartz aggregate in base coat Usually optional or absent
HOA Documentation Color compliance sheets available No documentation provided
Expected Service Life 10+ years with proper care 1–3 years before peeling or fading
Moisture Vapor Testing Pre-install assessment included Not performed - risk of delamination
