What Can Affect the Final Price

  • → Slab condition — heavy cracking, oil contamination, or an existing coating that must be removed adds prep time
  • → Finish type — standard color-chip systems are base pricing; metallic and full-broadcast finishes add $1–$3 / sq ft

DIY vs. Professional — The Real Math

Big-box store epoxy kits run $300–$700 and typically last 1–3 years. A properly installed ArmorClad floor lasts 15–20 years. When you divide the professional cost over its lifespan, it’s consistently the lower-cost option — and it won’t peel up when your hot tires roll in.
